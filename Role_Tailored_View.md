
# Role‑Tailored View: ServiceNow vs Azure DevOps vs Atlassian Jira

## Product Owner Perspective

**Primary Needs**
- Backlog clarity and prioritization
- Roadmaps and stakeholder visibility
- Requirements and acceptance criteria
- Collaborative documentation

**Best Fit: Jira + Confluence**
- Excellent agile backlog and sprint management
- Strong documentation and decision tracking
- Intuitive UI for non‑technical stakeholders

**Trade‑offs**
- Portfolio features cost extra
- Requires governance at scale

---

## Engineering Perspective

**Primary Needs**
- End‑to‑end DevOps workflow
- CI/CD automation
- Code‑to‑work‑item traceability
- Developer productivity

**Best Fit: Azure DevOps**
- Integrated repos, pipelines, and boards
- Strong testing and release management
- Native Azure and GitHub integration

**Trade‑offs**
- Weak documentation tooling
- Less friendly for business users

---

## IT Ops Perspective

**Primary Needs**
- Incident, problem, and change management
- Compliance and auditability
- Workflow automation
- Knowledge reuse

**Best Fit: ServiceNow**
- Industry‑leading ITSM and governance
- Enterprise‑grade automation
- Strong knowledge management

**Trade‑offs**
- High cost and complexity
- Not designed for fast product iteration
